GRIT Training Exercise

GRIT: Guardian Readiness and Integration Training

GRIT physically and mentally forges air and space cadets into capable warfighters and future Guardians through realistic training scenarios and space mission simulations. Integrating the AFFORGEN and SPAFORGEN deployment models, cadets engage in joint force deployment, space wargaming, and decision-making exercises, with direct mentorship from Guardians on planning and executing national space defense missions.

Operations

Day 01 - DELTA NOVEMBER: Focused on initial space defense strategies and deployment.

Day 02 - DARK CERBERUS: Advanced space operations and warfighting scenarios.
